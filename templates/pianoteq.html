Pianoteq is a realistic physical modelling engine developed by Moddart and approved by Steinway & Sons among others. It can simulate a wide range of instruments, like Grand Pianos, Electric Pianos, Clavichords, Harpas, Celestas, Vibraphones, Bells, etc. with a high degree of precision and realism. It's a pleasure to play it!

Zynthian SD images include the restricted trial version of "Pianoteq 6 Stage", that have some keys disabled and stop playing after 20 minutes.

If you already have a Pianoteq licence (Stage, Standard or Pro), on this page you can install the licensed binary and activate it with your license key. Also, you can upgrade to newer versions of the trial or licensed binaries, as well as install PTQ modules too.
